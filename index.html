About Us

Hi, dear travelers! Kazakhstan Guided Tours team is pleased to welcome you to our website. Our mission is to help foreign tourists not only see the beauty of Kazakhstan, but also get an unforgettable travel experience today and forever. We offer group and private nature sightseeing tours with outdoor activities providing all-inclusive services (transport, guide, food/drinks, accommodation, pick-up/drop-off) in Almaty region and Kazakhstan.

Why Choose Us

Our guides are tourist needs-oriented and responsive. They speak fluent English, so it is easy to communicate with them. All our vehicles are modern, comfortable, and equipped with AC for comfortable and cheerful long trips. We can't show scenes more beautiful than nature has created, and we can't take you further than the road allows. But we can create the most comfortable conditions for you, so you can fully enjoy the journey and don't care about anything. The full satisfaction of our tourists is a top priority for us. Therefore, we take into account all details, tourists' needs/wishes, and design the best tour products with excellent service to make your travel comfortable, unforgettable and full of positive emotions. If you are interested in our services, be sure you will get an unforgettable experience! We will be very happy to make Kazakhstan more accessible to you!
